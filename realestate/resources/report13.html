Home Inspections Just Don't Benefit Buyers
 

A home inspection is not only advantageous for the homebuyer but for you, the seller, as well.

If there are problems you’ll want to know about them. Not to fear, it might not be a deal killer for a buyer. But a home inspection will also be an independent third-party’s determination of the condition of the home before you’ve sold it. This is always good to have.
