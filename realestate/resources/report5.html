Home Inspections Just Don't Benefit Buyers
 

Once you have decided to purchase a home, the entire process is dependent upon the state of your financial health. What that means is that when the process is underway you should be very careful about any major credit purchases.

How much home you might be able to qualify for is a matter of ratios such as income to current credit payments. Each additional $100 per month in payments you take on in credit can take away approximately $10,000 from the purchase price of the home.

If you know you are going to purchase a home, you might consider delaying any large purchases such as a new car. This can go a long way to getting the home you want and one you have to “settle for” because you couldn’t qualify for “the one.”
