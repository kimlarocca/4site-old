Home Inspections Just Don't Benefit Buyers
 

It may seem like a silly question, but an honest evaluation will determine your strategy and assist your agent when the house is listed.

If you need a quick sale for whatever reason, then a buyer that offers you a great price, but can’t close for three months won’t help you achieve your goal. A lower price, but with a strongly qualified buyer willing to close quickly might just be the best deal for you as a seller.

It's always good to know how low you will go, in terms of a selling price. This will help to eliminate some of the offers that you find simply offensive or ridiculous. Even though you should consider all offers seriously and take into consideration the terms of each offer, sometimes, if you know the bottom line and are strict about it, you can save yourself time.

Setting realistic goals for your sale is an area where your agent can assist you. Knowing the market can be invaluable for you getting the best price in your time frame.
